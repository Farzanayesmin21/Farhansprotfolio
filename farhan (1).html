About Me

Designing identities that tell stories.

I'm Farhan, an independent logo designer with a passion for shaping how brands look, feel, and connect. Over the past eight years I've partnered with over 120 founders, agencies, and Fortune 500 teams to build identities that age well and stand out.

My process is collaborative and strategic — every mark, color, and curve is chosen with purpose. From mono-line minimalism to bold maximalist systems, I tailor every brand to its audience.
